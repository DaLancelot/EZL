About Us

Mission

At its core the mission of EZL begins with creating an global, multi-platform, non-toxic envirnoment for any and all players of any eSport to compete, learn, grow and much more together.

Vision

The EZL is a developing league that is much more than just a league. It began The hope is to take Esports to the traditional sports level. We also envision to one day change the perception of gaming to one day be respected, desired, and worked for not only by gamers but even by parents. The league will be able to provide an oppurtunity for everyone to earn and improve a Resume that they could use not only to rise in the League itself but even to use for other purposes. We also hope to provide an oppurtunity for those willing to learn not just how to play but how to do any of the tasks in the league such as coding, marketing, managing and much more. We hope to one day have each and every position paid in the future and have intern positions where you can learn, improve your resume and slowly rise to higher positions. All this will be occuring throughout many different platforms and all around the world. We also hope to build gaming houses for orphans all throughout the world as well as create a sponsorship for them to be able to go to college and much more. Come join us and help us achieve this together because we all know that everyone wants to change the world, but we are the few that know it can't be done without your help. No matter who you are, where you are, we will have a place and need for you. The vision is big, the dream is big, the hope is big and our hearts are even bigger.
