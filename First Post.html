GSL News

Below you will find updated news related to the GankStars League. The reporters will be writing amazing articles regarding every division, game, updates, and much more for the GSL.

The applications have been opened!

Come apply to be a part of the GSL as a member, captain, or have a position in the GSL at Apply. This will send in an application to the admins who will respond as quickly as possible. If you have not recieved a reply in 24 hours, please contact Skillz4Killz at Contact



Rank, skill, fame, status, all that is irrelevant. Focus on making relationships- those are what is really important and they last longer than anything else.



Although applications are open they will only be open for a limited time. Eventually applications will be closed and the date of closing will be announced in advance. However, it is important to keep in mind that the first applications will most obviously recieve the first attentions, so if you are hoping to recieve a position in the GSL, make sure to apply as soon as possible.

Furthermore, It is important to keep in mind that once the applications close, is 3 days before Draft Day. This is done to make sure that there are no last minute surprises applicants for the GankStars League, and allows every captain to have a fair amount of time to review and scout each and every applicant. Once the applications close, they will not be opened again, until the Draft is complete.

Afterwards the applications will once again be opened so that we may have anyone wishing to apply to the second season will be able to. Furthermore, once the season starts these applicants who applied late will become the free agent pool for the first season. This means that when a team needs a player due to an unfortunate reason confirmed by an admin, they will be able to recruit a player from the free agent pool.
